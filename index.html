Palos Verdes Spanish Learning Center

Palos Verdes Spanish Learning Center is a wonderful place to learn Spanish!

We are a highly experienced tutoring center with 12 years of instructing in the Palos Verdes community and an additional 20 years in a Spanish-speaking country.

Sign Up Today! Classes Starting Soon!

We believe Spanish can be a beautiful language and thus work very hard on making sure the students feel comfortable with it whether in reading, writing, singing, acting, art, cooking etc. We instruct kindergarten elementary, intermediate, high school students, college students and even homeschoolers! With some hard work and dedication, our students have proven results with high grades and most go on to receive a 5 on the AP exam. We offer one on one classes, group classes and Jr High School and High School Prep Classes.
